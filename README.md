# 🍫 Chocolate Store — Sales & Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive business intelligence project analyzing the sales 
and operations of a multi-country chocolate retail chain. 
Built using Power BI, the dashboard provides actionable insights 
across revenue, profit, customer behavior, and store performance 
for the years 2023 and 2024.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To identify key drivers of profitability and sales growth, 
understand customer behavior patterns, and provide 
data-driven strategic recommendations to improve 
overall business performance.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Analyzed
- Total Revenue & Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Average Chocolates per Order
- Sales & Profit Performance Trends
- Top Countries & Months by Profit

---

## 🔍 Analysis Areas

### 1. Sales & Profit Performance
- Identified top-performing brands and product categories
- Analyzed the relationship between sales volume and profit

### 2. Customer Behavior
- Segmented orders by gender and loyalty status
- Identified seasonal patterns in customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluated the impact of repeat/loyal customers on revenue

### 3. Store Performance
- Compared store count vs. sales and profit by country
- Identified Airport stores as the highest-performing store type
- Tracked store expansion trends across 2023–2024

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Slight but consistent year-over-year profit growth
- Airport stores generate the highest orders and profit
- Strong gross profit margin across the portfolio
- High demand concentrated in specific chocolate brands/categories
- Loyal vs. non-loyal customers are nearly equal — retention opportunity
- Sales are gender-independent

---

## ✅ Strategic Recommendations
- Focus on top-profit products and categories
- Launch loyalty and customer retention programs
- Expand retail store presence in high-performing locations
- Boost online sales through advertising and bundle offers
- Capitalize on peak sales months with targeted promotions
- Implement cost control measures for better net margin visibility
